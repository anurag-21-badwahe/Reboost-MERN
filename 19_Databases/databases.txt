Q1) What is no-sql database?
Ans)NoSQL, or non-relational database, is a database management approach that uses a variety of data models instead of the tabular relations model used in relational databases.

Here are some types of NoSQL databases: Key-value pair, Document-oriented, Column-oriented, Graph-based, and Time series.
